Content

Home page

The home page will have a picture of Mina with a summary of what her company does underneath. Her company provides esthetician services with a professional atmosphere and a personal touch. Mina will always aim to have her customers leave feeling beautiful and confident. The home page will also include the names of the professional product lines she uses such as Brow Code, Esthemax, and Farmhouse Fresh.

Services

The services Mina provides include but are not limited to, eyebrow tinting, makeup, eyelash lifts, and facials. Eyebrow tinting is where semi-permanent dye is used to darken your eyebrows to make them more prominent. Makeup looks can range from natural to full face. Lash lifts are used to enhance your natural lashes and make them pop. Facials help clear and moisturize your skin. Facials help your skin feel healthier.
